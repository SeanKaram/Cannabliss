About Our Company

West Coast Cannabliss is a new company based in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Our mission is to bring an artisanal twist to frozen marijuana edible goods. We do so while providing an all natural, quality product with nothing but the best ingredients.

The cooperation of a few industry professionals with creative minds, a real love for food, and especially, enjoy partaking in recreational THC, have come together to offer a delicious treat which tastes true to it's respective flavour. With no marijuana flavouring ,you can now enjoy a delicious Marijuana frozen dessert with a chill...ing after effect.
