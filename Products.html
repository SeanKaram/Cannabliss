What is Chillato?

Chillato is an artisanal gelato infused with THC Distillate. Chillato is made in both dairy and non dairy (coconut milk) options, we offer an infused Chocolate Gelato using Fine Belgian Chocolate and an infused Vanilla Gelato which uses real Madagascar Vanilla Bean.

Each product is available in either a 15mg, 25mg or 50mg dose of 99% Pure THC distillate, free of Terpenes and other chemical ingredients.
